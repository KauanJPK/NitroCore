## NitroCore Vision
NitroCore exists to make Minecraft more efficient without changing how it is played.

</div>

---

**NitroCore is not just an FPS mod.**

- NitroCore is a CPU optimization engine designed primarily for Minecraft Forge 1.7.10.

- The project focuses on reducing CPU usage on unnecessary particles, animations, effects, modded or vanilla. Indirectly
increases the FPS.
- The project focuses on reducing unnecessary CPU usage caused by particles, animations, visual effects and modded rendering. 
Higher FPS is a consequence, not the primary objective.


---

**Every optimization must satisfy at least one of the following goals:**

- Reduce CPU time.
- Reduce memory allocations.
- Reduce unnecessary rendering like chunks and entities.
- Reduce unnecessary game logic, animations, exaggerated particles and machines process in case of large modpacks, large and heavily modded Minecraft modpacks. 
- Reduce battery usage (Mobile or Notebook).
- Reduce FPS stutters.
- NitroCore never changes server logic or gameplay mechanics. Every optimization is strictly client-side.
- Give players full control over every optimization.


---

**Rules:**

- No unnecessary allocations.
- No reflection in hot paths.
- No per-tick overhead unless strictly necessary.
- Configuration is loaded once and cached.
- Mixins should remain as small as possible.
- Every optimization must be measurable.

---

**Long-Term Vision**

- NitroCore aims to become a complete optimization platform for Minecraft.

- The project starts with Minecraft Forge 1.7.10 but is designed with long-term evolution in mind, allowing future support for newer versions and platforms while preserving the same philosophy:

**Performance Without Compromise.**
